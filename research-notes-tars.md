## Key Industry Shift
- From hardware competition to "brain" competition
- 2024: Focus on body capabilities (movement, joints, biomimicry)
- 2026: Focus on brain capabilities (understanding, decision-making, generalization)
- Quote: "Hardware is存量博弈, brain decides增量定价权" (Hardware is zero-sum game, brain decides incremental pricing power)
